  • About

    Welcome to Gigs that Matter.

    The purpose of this webpage is to provide quick access to upcoming concerts of your favorite bands and artists on Last.fm.

    All you need to do is enter your Last.fm username below and hit the search button. There is no login required and all of the remaining settings are optional.

    The form beneath is initialized with my own Last.fm username JogoMarbach so you can give it a try right away.
